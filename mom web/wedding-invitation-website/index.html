Story of Service

In the past 30+ years of working as Nursing Officer from Amar Jain hospital to SMS hospital, this journey was full of God's grace and mercy. Countless night, evening and morning duties and operations. Family like doctors and staff of Bangad Ward of Neurosurgery department, from joining to retirement supported me and was just a call away. Unlimited service with selflessness and gratitude for this lifetime opportunity. SMS YOU WILL BE MISSED, MY OT YOU WILL BE MISSED. As if a long period of my life is coming to an end, but goodbyes are never the end.

“Just so you know, doctors are here to diagnose, not heal. We heal.”

Biography

Saroj George was born and brought up amidst of 4 sisters and widowed father, the journey of leaving her hometown and moving Jaipur was not so smooth, when her husband had fatal accident, she had to sleep with empty stomach and was struggling financially too. When she came into faith and believed Jesus Christ as her savior, he opened every door of blessing, within span of time, she got Government Job after working in 9 years of private sector. Her life is full of blessings and testimony, she and her husband runs "Helping Hands" which helps patients to seek early medical help in SMS hospital. and is blessed with two daughters Sakshi: Journalist and Ayushi: Engineer. During the span of 27+ of service, she was promoted to OT IN CHARGE and can been celebrating every event such as Dressed as santa, giving chocolates, and praying for the patients. Indeed the purpose for which Lord has kept her in the hospital, has been fulfilled by her fully. Let's celebrate the act of service together.
